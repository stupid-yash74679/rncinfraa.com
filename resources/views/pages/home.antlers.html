About Us

RNC Infraa Private Limited is a leader in prefab construction, delivering high-quality, sustainable, and cost-efficient modular infrastructure. Our prefab solutions reduce construction time, minimize costs, and enhance durability, making infrastructure smarter and more efficient. From design to installation, we ensure seamless execution, helping businesses, industries, and communities build the future faster.
Why Choose RNC Infraa?
With years of expertise in prefab infrastructure, we specialize in customized, durable, and scalable solutions. Our prefab buildings are designed to optimize space, reduce costs, and enhance energy efficiency, ensuring long-term sustainability for businesses and communities.
Our Process
Our prefab construction process follows global standards, ensuring faster project completion and minimal waste. From design and material selection to modular assembly and installation, every step is optimized for efficiency, quality, and sustainability.
Our Vision & Mission
Our mission is to make prefab infrastructure the standard for modern construction, delivering eco-friendly, high-performance modular buildings that transform industries. We aim to revolutionize construction with smart and scalable prefab solutions that drive global progress.

Introducing Hybrid Prefab Construction by RNC Infraa, a cutting-edge building solution that combines speed with enhanced design flexibility. Ideal for projects requiring rapid deployment without compromising on aesthetic appeal or structural integrity, Hybrid Prefab excels in delivering versatile spaces for a wide array of applications, from commercial to community needs.

Leveraging a smart blend of prefabricated components and on-site construction techniques, Hybrid Prefab Construction offers a streamlined building process that significantly reduces project timelines and costs. This method is particularly advantageous for creating modern, functional spaces quickly, making it a superior choice over traditional building for projects demanding both efficiency and design adaptability.

  • Hybrid Office Buildings for Urban Expansions – Rapid office space solutions for quickly growing businesses in urban and suburban areas.
  • Hybrid Workforce Housing for Skilled Labor – Comfortable and efficient temporary housing for skilled workers on diverse project sites.
  • Hybrid Guest Lodges for Eco-Tourism Resorts – Stylish and sustainable guest accommodations for rapidly developing eco-tourism destinations.
  • Hybrid Canteens & Cafeterias for Educational Institutions – Durable and quickly installed dining facilities for schools and universities experiencing growth.
  • Hybrid Retail Units for Pop-Up Commercial Zones – Modern and adaptable retail spaces for creating temporary or seasonal shopping areas.
